## Business Challenge

Northwind Financial Services has experienced rapid growth over the last five years.

The existing on-premises infrastructure is difficult to maintain, lacks centralised identity management and does not adequately support hybrid working.

The business has approved a migration to Microsoft Azure and Microsoft 365 to improve security, scalability and operational efficiency.
